修改目前theme的配置文件(your_hexo_folder/themes/next/_config.yml)

{% codeblock %}
 # Place your favicon.ico to /source directory.
 favicon: [your_new_icon.png]
{% endcodeblock %}

依照註解，將欲顯示的icon [your_new_icon.png]，放到your_hexo_folder/source下就好
